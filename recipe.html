Honey-Soy Glazed Chicken

This 3-Ingredient Honey-Soy Glazed Chicken features tender, juicy chicken thighs coated in a rich, glossy sauce. It delivers a perfect balance of bold, savory soy sauce and deep, sweet honey. The chicken sears beautifully to create crisp edges while maintaining a succulent, tender interior. This sticky, syrupy glaze clings perfectly to every single bite. Because it requires only three pantry staples and a single frying pan, it moves from your kitchen counter to the dinner table in under 15 minutes. It is an effortless, fast meal that pairs beautifully with basic sides like white rice, noodles, or steamed broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 boneless chicken thighs (skin-on or skinless)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Place the deboned chicken in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat to render out the oil. Sear for 2-3 minutes per side until nicely colored.
  2. Remove the chicken to a cutting board and slice it into bite-sized cubes
  3. Return the chicken to the pan, and pour in the soy sauce and honey
  4. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to medium. Simmer and stir continuously until the sauce becomes a thick, syrupy glaze (about 5-8 minutes).
  5. Serve hot as-is or over a bowl of steamed rice.

Note: Best enjoyed with the ones you love!
